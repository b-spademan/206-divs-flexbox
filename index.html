I was born and raised in Toronto, Canada. After finishing university I followed my heart to the mountains where what was supposed to be 6 months working at the Lake Louise Ski Resort, turned into five years! I may be a big city girl, but the small mountain towns is where my heart is.

I LOVE the outdoors. Whether it be hiking, taking it easy on the beach or shredding the mountain. I love adventure and discovering the beauty of the natural world.

Fun Fact: At the age of 5 I started taking cello lessons, and in highschool went to an Arts school! Although I am classically trained, in university I had a go at playing cover songs at the local pub on open mic nights with a friend. That was fun!
